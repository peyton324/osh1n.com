I (Peyton Dunham) was born on August 17th, 1998 in Atlanta, Georgia. I was raised with my brother Blake and sister Lauren by my parents, Cindy and Gerry. Most of my childhood was spent at the park watching my dad’s (and brother’s) football team or playing baseball. Playing all the way until college, baseball took up most of my time. In between school, practices, and games, I was able to teach myself how to play the guitar, drums, and piano. I brought my knowledge to the computer in the 10th grade when I could save up for Logic Pro. After high school, I was able to allocate more time into learning the world of music. Eventually, I purchased better plugins and equipment, opening new doors to my progress. Finally seeing my passion for music, I decided to take my abilities public. That’s where “0sh1n” comes from because a stage name is imperative. Although rap, hip-hop, and fire trap beats are my most common projects, tropical house is my favorite to both write; it’s just impossible to not feel happy listening to it.
